## Corrections made

保留 Lichtenstein標準地位，同時更新 adequate flat-mesh coverage、nerve recognition、CPIP、antibiotic個別化、避免 plug與 pain-guided early recovery。

## Residual uncertainty

Mesh weight、fixation與 nerve strategy仍受 product、technique及 surgeon experience影響；CPIP treatment需 phenotype與 specialist resources。**10/10只代表通過本專案 audit threshold，不保證個別病人的臨床正確性或安全性。**
