## Corrections made

以 ASCCP risk thresholds取代線性 Pap-to-cone流程；加入 visible cancer lesion override、pregnancy限制、CIN2／3差異、long-term surveillance及 hysteroscopy優於 blind D&C的適用情境。

## Residual uncertainty
