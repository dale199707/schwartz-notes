## Corrections made

修正 fissure surface anatomy 的固定化敘述、補入 fissure variants與 segmental vessel principles、將 pleural procedures擴充為 ultrasound-based safety workflow，並限定 JCOG/CALGB sublobar evidence的 tumor size、location與 nodal條件。

## Residual uncertainty

Segmentectomy、wedge、lobectomy與 carcinoid resection extent仍須依 pathology、margin、nodal assessment與 MDT決定；fissure與 vessel variants須以個別 CT／3D reconstruction及術中視野確認。**10/10是稽核門檻，不保證所有臨床情境均正確。**
