## Corrections made

移除固定 CEA間隔、extrahepatic disease絕對禁忌、1–2 cm margin與「多發即不能切／反而做 lobectomy」等過時簡化；改用 MDT、disease biology、complete clearance、functional FLR、IOUS、R0與 perioperative therapy shared decision框架。

## Residual uncertainty

PSR與 anatomic resection比較多為 observational evidence，受 tumor location、surgeon selection與 systemic therapy影響；CRLM策略應依最新 oncology與 HPB MDT決策。**10/10只代表通過本專案 audit threshold，不保證個別病人的臨床正確性或安全性。**
