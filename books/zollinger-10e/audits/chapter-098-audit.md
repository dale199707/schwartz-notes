## Corrections made

補齊 injury pattern、delayed presentation、CT urography／pyelography、immediate與 delayed repair分流、reconstructive ladder、blood-supply preservation及 prophylactic stent限制。

## Residual uncertainty

Complex long-segment reconstruction、stent duration與 follow-up imaging仍依 defect、radiation／prior surgery、感染與 reconstructive expertise不同。**10/10只代表通過本專案 audit threshold，不保證個別病人的臨床正確性或安全性。**
