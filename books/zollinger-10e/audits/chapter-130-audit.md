## Corrections made

補 recipient-bed適應性、graft-take生理、STSG／FTSG tradeoff、NPWT evidence、moist donor dressing與 early mobilization。

## Residual uncertainty

Fixation pressure、dressing duration與 mobilization protocols因 wound、anatomic site與 device差異而異，meta-analysis具異質性。**10/10只代表通過本專案 audit threshold，不保證個別病人的臨床正確性或安全性。**
