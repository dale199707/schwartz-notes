## Corrections made

補 contemporary endovascular／hybrid定位、donor與 recipient assessment、extra-anatomic durability tradeoff、dynamic tunnel與完整 PAD secondary prevention。

## Residual uncertainty

Femorofemoral bypass的 comparative evidence多為 observational且受 indication bias影響；local endovascular與 open expertise會改變選擇。**10/10只代表通過本專案 audit threshold，不保證個別病人的臨床正確性或安全性。**
