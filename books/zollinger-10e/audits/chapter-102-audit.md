## Corrections made

將 component separation定位為 complex reconstruction工具；補上 anterior wound burden、posterior／TAR alternative、midline closure、retromuscular mesh與 contaminated-field synthetic mesh RCT。

## Residual uncertainty

Advanced abdominal-wall reconstruction缺少足夠 randomized head-to-head evidence；gross infection、unstable emergency與不同 mesh plane不可直接套用 elective RCT。**10/10只代表通過本專案 audit threshold，不保證個別病人的臨床正確性或安全性。**
