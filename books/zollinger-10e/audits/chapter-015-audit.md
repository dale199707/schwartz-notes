## Corrections made

移除固定肋間、三層樓 clearance、routine ABG、mandatory epidural、≥32 Fr／兩根 chest tubes與固定POD拔管；改以2025 functional risk、ERAS multimodal care及2023–2024 criteria-based drain management重寫。

## Residual uncertainty

Thoracotomy approach、regional analgesia與 drain strategy高度依賴實際 procedure、anticoagulation、lung quality與 center protocol；fitness thresholds不能脫離 MDT判讀。**10/10只代表本章通過本專案 audit threshold，不是個別病人安全或臨床正確性的保證。**
