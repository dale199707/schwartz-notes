## Corrections made

把 indication清單改為 decision-focused DL；補 stable-patient requirement、negative-test限制、gastric cytology與 pancreatic CT-first selective pathway，並下修 chronic-pain adhesiolysis的普遍性。

## Residual uncertainty

Staging-laparoscopy yield受 disease stage、imaging quality、neoadjuvant strategy與 local oncology pathway影響；SAGES broad guideline較舊，疾病特定建議應優先查最新完整版。**10/10只代表本章通過本專案 audit threshold，不是個別病人安全或臨床正確性的保證。**
