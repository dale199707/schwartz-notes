## Corrections made

將 McVay由 routine direct repair改為 historical／selected salvage；補上 femoral-vein與 corona-mortis風險、tension判讀、combined defect檢查及 contemporary alternatives。

## Residual uncertainty

McVay在 infected mesh explant或 resource-limited情境缺少高品質 contemporary comparative data，應由 hernia specialist依 anatomy決策。**10/10只代表通過本專案 audit threshold，不保證個別病人的臨床正確性或安全性。**
