## Corrections made

將原書 laparoscopic IPOM改為 selected approach；補上 CT planning、retromuscular preference、fascial closure、enterotomy決策、seroma observation與 emergency staged strategy。

## Residual uncertainty

Robotic／minimally invasive retromuscular technique、mesh overlap與 fixation仍受 defect anatomy、device及 surgeon expertise影響；emergency recommendation確定性偏低。**10/10只代表通過本專案 audit threshold，不保證個別病人的臨床正確性或安全性。**
