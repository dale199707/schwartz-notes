# Chapter 068: Excision of Pilonidal Sinus

## 核心整理

- Pilonidal disease從 asymptomatic pits、acute abscess到 chronic sinus，處理強度應匹配 disease burden。
- 確認 midline pits、acute abscess、chronic sinus與 differential diagnosis；asymptomatic pits可觀察。
- Acute abscess先 incision and drainage；需要 definitive excision時避免 midline closure，off-midline closure較佳；selected disease可用 minimally invasive approach。
- Acute abscess I&D；definitive excision如需 closure採 off-midline，選擇性 limited disease可 minimally invasive。
- 避免 midline tension/cleft wound與過廣 tissue loss；評估 recurrent/nonhealing原因。
- Symptomatic chronic/recurrent disease且理解 wound/recurrence trade-off者。
- Asymptomatic pits不需 prophylactic excision；active abscess不要直接做複雜 closure。
- Open healing較慢但無 closure tension；off-midline closure恢復較快且優於 midline closure。
- Routine methylene blue、廣泛 shaving或固定 excision深度缺乏普遍必要性；hair control與 wound strategy個別化。
- Spreading cellulitis、systemic illness、recurrent abscess或不癒合傷口需重新評估 infection與 diagnosis。

## Clinical pearl

- Pilonidal disease從 asymptomatic pits、acute abscess到 chronic sinus，處理強度應匹配 disease burden。
- Acute abscess I&D；definitive excision如需 closure採 off-midline，選擇性 limited disease可 minimally invasive。
- Asymptomatic pits不需 prophylactic excision；active abscess不要直接做複雜 closure。
- Spreading cellulitis、systemic illness、recurrent abscess或不癒合傷口需重新評估 infection與 diagnosis。

## 後續證據更新

- ESCP 2024取代原稿較舊 meta-analysis並反對 midline closure。
- ESCP 2024明確偏好 off-midline closure並避免 primary midline closure；minimally invasive方法適合 selected limited disease。
- 適用限制：guideline提供 population-level方向；實際處置仍受 disease stage、physiology、local expertise與病人偏好影響，不能把單一數字當作絕對門檻。
