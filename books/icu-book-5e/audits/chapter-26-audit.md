## Corrections and residual uncertainty

Integrated the June 2026 ATS guideline, separated absolute airway emergencies from relative NIV limitations, reframed HACOR as risk support, and made post-extubation use risk-based. Interface choice and NIV failure thresholds remain population- and system-dependent.
