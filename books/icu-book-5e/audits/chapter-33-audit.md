## Corrections and residual uncertainty

Replaced oversimplified contraction language, integrated urine chloride with blood pressure and congestion, and constrained acetazolamide and acid-infusion claims. Urine chloride remains dynamic after diuretics and fluid therapy.

## Re-audit conclusion

All ten questions are `SUPPORTED` after revision. This 10/10 score is an editorial threshold, not a guarantee of clinical correctness; severe alkalemia requires serial electrolytes, ventilation assessment and cause-specific expert management.
