## Corrections and residual uncertainty

Converted reflex testing to a source-directed evaluation, qualified biomarkers, separated comfort treatment from outcome claims and restored selected physical-cooling indications with shivering control. Temperature thresholds and investigation intensity still require adaptation for neutropenia, transplant, acute brain injury and local epidemiology.

## Re-audit conclusion

All ten questions are `SUPPORTED` after revision. This 10/10 score is an editorial threshold, not a guarantee of clinical correctness; shock, hyperthermia syndromes and severely immunocompromised hosts require immediate condition-specific protocols.
