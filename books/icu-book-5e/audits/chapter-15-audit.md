## Corrections and residual uncertainty

Corrected the REBOA evidence, constrained whole-blood and TXA claims, preserved PROPPR endpoint hierarchy, and made calcium/fibrinogen instructions product- and measurement-aware. The best REBOA subgroup and the comparative long-term benefit of whole blood remain uncertain.
