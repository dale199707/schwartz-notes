## Corrections and residual uncertainty

Clarified that KDIGO 2026 is not final, recalibrated contrast and antibiotic causal claims, constrained fluid/diuretic use, and defined when delayed KRT evidence does not apply. Individual kidney risk and local imaging/KRT resources still require bedside judgment.

## Re-audit conclusion

All ten questions are `SUPPORTED` after revision. This 10/10 score is an editorial threshold, not a guarantee of clinical correctness; AKI trajectory, emergencies, drug exposure and volume state must be reassessed serially.
