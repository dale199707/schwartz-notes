## Corrections and residual uncertainty

Added the formal PMV definition, removed certainty around early tracheostomy benefits, clarified that tracheostomy does not guarantee speech/swallowing/liberation, and strengthened WIPO and shared-decision framing. Recovery trajectory remains highly diagnosis- and baseline-function dependent.

## Re-audit conclusion

All ten questions are `SUPPORTED` after revision. This 10/10 score is an editorial threshold, not a guarantee of clinical correctness; prognosis and tracheostomy timing require individualized multidisciplinary review.
