## Corrections and residual uncertainty

Added the 2024 AARC positions on RSBI, daily timing, SBT method and stable FiO₂; removed an unsupported T-piece preference; and incorporated ATS 2026 post-extubation risk stratification. No single SBT or airway metric guarantees extubation success.

## Re-audit conclusion

All ten questions are `SUPPORTED` after revision. This 10/10 score is an editorial threshold, not a guarantee of clinical correctness; extubation requires current airway, secretion, neurologic and cardiopulmonary assessment.
