## Corrections and residual uncertainty

Updated CDC surveillance to 2026, added diagnostic sampling and antibiotic timeout, removed automatic longer treatment for nonfermenters, and bounded REGARD-VAP. VAP diagnosis remains probabilistic and local resistance patterns remain essential.

## Re-audit conclusion

All ten questions are `SUPPORTED` after revision. This 10/10 score is an editorial threshold, not a guarantee of clinical correctness; microbiology, severity, immune status and local stewardship guidance govern treatment.
