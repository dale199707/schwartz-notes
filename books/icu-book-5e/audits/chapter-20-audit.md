## Corrections and residual uncertainty

Restricted potent P2Y12 preference to PCI, made DAPT duration bleeding-risk dependent, clarified the shock revascularization exception and replaced a blanket type 2 MI antithrombotic rule with mechanism-based assessment. Optimal type 2 MI testing and therapy remain major evidence gaps.

## Re-audit conclusion

All ten questions are `SUPPORTED` after revision. This score is an editorial threshold; local STEMI systems, cath-lab protocols, contraindications and cardiology judgment control patient care.
