## Corrections and residual uncertainty

Updated the acute algorithm to 2025, restricted adenosine in wide-QRS rhythm, changed sepsis anticoagulation from a negative rule to guideline-level uncertainty, and added post-discharge surveillance. ICU-specific rate-vs-rhythm and anticoagulation trials remain insufficient.

## Re-audit conclusion

All ten questions are `SUPPORTED` after revision. This score does not replace rhythm interpretation, ACLS training, cardiology consultation or local drug/electrical-cardioversion protocols.
