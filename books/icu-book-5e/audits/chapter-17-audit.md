## Corrections and residual uncertainty

Converted fluid and anaphylaxis observation rules from rigid thresholds to conditional, response-based decisions; separated confirmed streptococcal TSS from empirical staphylococcal coverage. Steroid regimen details, optimal post-bolus fluid strategy and IVIG benefit in STSS remain uncertain.

## Re-audit conclusion

All ten questions are `SUPPORTED` after revision. This score is an editorial threshold; use local sepsis, anaphylaxis, antimicrobial and surgical-source-control protocols.
