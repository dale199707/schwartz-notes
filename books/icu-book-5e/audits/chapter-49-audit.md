## Corrections and residual uncertainty

Added the missing NUTRIREA-3 outcomes, distinguished stable from uncontrolled shock, qualified GRV practice across guidelines and operationalized intolerance/aspiration/diarrhea care. The safe vasopressor dose and optimal early calorie-protein combination remain uncertain and cannot be reduced to one threshold.

## Re-audit conclusion

All ten questions are `SUPPORTED` after revision. This 10/10 score is an editorial threshold, not a guarantee of clinical correctness; worsening shock or abdominal signs during feeding require immediate bedside reassessment for ischemia.
