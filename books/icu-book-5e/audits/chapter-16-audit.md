## Corrections and residual uncertainty

Clarified SCAI stage B, separated stable multivessel ACS from AMI-CS, bounded vasoactive claims, and framed each device by randomized benefit, harm and eligibility. Optimal device timing, MCS selection outside DanGer-like STEMI-CS, and the best invasive-hemodynamic strategy remain uncertain.
