## Corrections and residual uncertainty

Replaced a universal day-seven rule with nutrition-risk and route-specific timing, supplied exact EPaNIC outcomes/limits, removed an oversimplified infection mechanism and added substrate ceilings, refeeding response and liver differential. The best timing for high-risk patients and first-week supplemental PN remains a cross-guideline controversy.

## Re-audit conclusion

All ten questions are `SUPPORTED` after revision. This 10/10 score is an editorial threshold, not a guarantee of clinical correctness; PN requires daily multidisciplinary review of indication, access, metabolic tolerance and enteral recovery.
