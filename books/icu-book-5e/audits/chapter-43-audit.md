## Corrections and residual uncertainty

Made heat-stroke cooling immediate and protocolized, removed routine antipyretic/dantrolene use, qualified the hypothermia slogan and updated ECLS triage to HOPE plus VA-ECMO. The fastest feasible cooling method and transport decision still depend on patient access, airway, geography and local capability.

## Re-audit conclusion

All ten questions are `SUPPORTED` after revision. This 10/10 score is an editorial threshold, not a guarantee of clinical correctness; heat stroke, malignant hyperthermia and hypothermic arrest are protocol-driven emergencies requiring immediate specialist support.
