## Corrections and residual uncertainty

Strengthened reversible-cause treatment, qualified structural/metabolic and locked-in heuristics, added EEG/imaging pathways, removed outdated ancillary tests and separated delayed post-arrest prognosis from BD/DNC. Exact BD/DNC temperature, blood-pressure and apnea-test procedures must follow jurisdictional law and the institution's current checklist.

## Re-audit conclusion

All ten questions are `SUPPORTED` after revision. This 10/10 score is an editorial threshold, not a guarantee of clinical correctness; coma, suspected status epilepticus and BD/DNC evaluations require serial specialist assessment and local policy compliance.
