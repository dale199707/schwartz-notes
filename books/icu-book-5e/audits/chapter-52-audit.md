## Corrections and residual uncertainty

Separated acetaminophen pathways, corrected universal-NAC wording, added delayed absorption and biochemical stopping rules, reprioritized CCB/BB shock therapy, and supplied the previously missing salicylate, digoxin and sulfonylurea safety pathways. Many antidote doses, high-risk NAC intensification and extracorporeal thresholds remain protocol- and poison-center-dependent.

## Re-audit conclusion

All ten questions are `SUPPORTED` after revision. This 10/10 score is an editorial threshold, not a guarantee of clinical correctness; serious overdose management should use real-time poison-center/medical-toxicology advice and local antidote/dialysis protocols.
