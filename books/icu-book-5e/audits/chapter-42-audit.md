## Corrections and residual uncertainty

Corrected bacteriuria timing, required a compatible syndrome, added new-catheter sampling after >2 weeks, stratified duration and narrowed candiduria treatment. Empiric drugs and imaging thresholds remain dependent on local resistance, shock physiology and suspected obstruction.

## Re-audit conclusion

All ten questions are `SUPPORTED` after revision. This 10/10 score is an editorial threshold, not a guarantee of clinical correctness; suspected infected obstruction or septic shock needs immediate source control and locally appropriate therapy.
