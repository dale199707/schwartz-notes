## Corrections and residual uncertainty

Removed the blanket suggestion to omit anaerobic therapy, shortened therapy after adequate source control, restricted empiric antifungal use and distinguished new sterile-site specimens from old drains. Exact source-control timing and empiric regimen remain anatomy-, physiology- and local-resistance dependent.

## Re-audit conclusion

All ten questions are `SUPPORTED` after revision. This 10/10 score is an editorial threshold, not a guarantee of clinical correctness; unstable cIAI requires immediate surgical, interventional, antimicrobial and critical-care coordination.
