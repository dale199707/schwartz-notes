## Corrections and residual uncertainty

Added tonicity/method classification, severe-symptom bolus targets, ODS risk limits and rescue strategy, and separated acute from chronic hypernatremia. Adult hypernatremia rate evidence is observational, so serial sodium and cause-specific adjustment remain essential.

## Re-audit conclusion

All ten questions are `SUPPORTED` after revision. This 10/10 score is an editorial threshold, not a guarantee of clinical correctness; symptomatic dysnatremia requires frequent laboratory monitoring and protocolized specialist oversight.
