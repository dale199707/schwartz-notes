## Corrections and residual uncertainty

Separated calorimetry accuracy from outcome benefit, made calorie targets phase- and method-specific, led with EFFORT's primary result, qualified AKI subgroup signals and limited refeeding criteria to a consensus framework. Optimal protein by illness phase, KRT and rehabilitation remains unresolved.

## Re-audit conclusion

All ten questions are `SUPPORTED` after revision. This 10/10 score is an editorial threshold, not a guarantee of clinical correctness; ICU nutrition must be reassessed daily with dietitian, pharmacy and critical-care input.
