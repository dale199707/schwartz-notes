## Corrections and residual uncertainty

Updated septic-shock steroids to SSC 2026, removed binary CIRCI testing, strengthened adrenal-crisis sequencing, made NTIS uncertainty explicit and added high-risk cardiac precautions to thyroid-storm beta blockade. CIRCI biomarkers, optimal thyroid-storm regimens and T3 use in myxedema coma remain supported mainly by low-certainty evidence and expert guidance.

## Re-audit conclusion

All ten questions are `SUPPORTED` after revision. This 10/10 score is an editorial threshold, not a guarantee of clinical correctness; endocrine emergencies require rapid treatment plus endocrinology/critical-care review and patient-specific cardiac/hemodynamic adjustment.
