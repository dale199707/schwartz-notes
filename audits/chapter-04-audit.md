## Re-audit conclusion

After revision, all ten questions are `SUPPORTED`. The chapter now distinguishes product contents from modern indications, makes transfusion thresholds population specific, and adds reversal/diagnostic safety gates.

Acute MI transfusion strategy and DOAC reversal continue to evolve. A 10/10 audit score is a quality threshold, not a guarantee or a replacement for current blood-bank and specialty protocols.
