## Re-audit conclusion

After revision, all ten questions are `SUPPORTED`. The chapter now separates emergency targets from daily correction limits, conditions acute hyperkalemia therapies appropriately, and treats fluid choice as context dependent.

Residual uncertainty remains in optimal perioperative fluid targets and patient-specific sodium correction limits. A 10/10 audit score is a reproducible quality threshold, not a guarantee of clinical correctness or a substitute for current local protocols.
