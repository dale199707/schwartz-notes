## Re-audit conclusion

After revision, all ten questions are `SUPPORTED`. The chapter now treats unstable trauma as a source- and physiology-driven pathway instead of a set of unconditional binary rules.

Pelvic sequencing, REBOA use, and TBI VTE timing remain resource- and protocol-dependent. A 10/10 audit score is a quality threshold, not a guarantee or a substitute for ATLS and local trauma protocols.
