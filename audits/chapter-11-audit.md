## Re-audit conclusion

After revision, all ten questions are `SUPPORTED`. The chapter now uses contemporary Banff phenotypes, does not treat crossmatch or AMR therapy as binary, and includes actionable BK surveillance.

Transplant allocation and immunosuppression are organ-, country-, and center-specific. A 10/10 audit score is a quality threshold, not a replacement for transplant-team protocols.
