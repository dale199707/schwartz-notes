## Re-audit conclusion

After revision, all ten questions are `SUPPORTED`. The chapter no longer uses a premature tensile-strength timeline, a universal bacterial-count threshold, or routine vitamin A advice.

Chronic-wound diagnosis and device choice remain etiology specific. A 10/10 audit score is a quality threshold, not a substitute for vascular, infection, and multidisciplinary assessment.
