## Re-audit conclusion

After revision, all ten questions are `SUPPORTED`. The chapter now distinguishes guideline-backed recommendations from hypotheses or associative biomarkers and states the main applicability limits.

Residual uncertainty remains around optimal ICU protein dosing, the clinical definition of PICS, and fluid-volume individualization. A 10/10 audit score is a reproducible quality threshold, not a guarantee of clinical correctness or a substitute for the latest full guideline and local protocol.
